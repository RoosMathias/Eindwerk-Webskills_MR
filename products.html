Nespresso

Nespresso machines brew espresso and coffee from coffee capsules (or pods in machines for home or professional use), a type of pre-apportioned single-use container, or reusable capsules (pods), of ground coffee beans, sometimes with added flavorings.

Filter

Coffee filter machines work with coffee filters. This allows you to put your own grounded coffee beans in to the filter. With this you can make your own authentic coffee, just the way you want it.

Senseo

Using a Senseo requires no hard work on your part. All you have to do is insert a coffee pod and select your cup size. It includes two pod holders, you can use one if you want to brew a single cup, or both holders to brew two pods at the same time
